E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis Report

1. Project Title

E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis

2. Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, payment preferences, customer satisfaction, and delivery performance using an e-commerce sales dataset. The analysis aims to generate meaningful business insights and provide data-driven recommendations for improving sales and customer experience.

3. Dataset Overview

- Total Records: 17,049
- Total Features: 18
- Data Types: Integer, Float, Boolean, and Object
- Missing Values: None
- Dataset Status: Clean and ready for analysis

4. Tools and Technologies

- Python
- NumPy
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab

5. Data Preparation

- Imported required libraries.
- Loaded the dataset successfully.
- Checked data structure and summary statistics.
- Verified that there were no missing values.
- Removed the unnecessary Order_ID column before performing analysis.

6. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Customer Analysis

- Female customers contribute the highest overall sales.
- Customers aged 25–45 years are the most active buyers.

Geographic Analysis

- Istanbul, Ankara, and Izmir generate the highest sales.

Product Performance

- Sports, Beauty, and Books have the highest purchase volume.
- Electronics and Home & Garden generate the highest total revenue.

Payment Analysis

- Credit Card and Debit Card are the most preferred payment methods in both transaction count and total sales value.

Device Analysis

- Most customers purchase products using mobile devices.

Customer Satisfaction

- The majority of customers give 5-star ratings, indicating high customer satisfaction.

Delivery Performance

- Most orders are delivered within 4–5 days, showing efficient delivery performance.

7. Key Business Insights

- The business has a strong customer base and healthy sales performance.
- Female customers and the 25–45 age group represent the most valuable customer segment.
- High-value product categories contribute significantly to revenue.
- Mobile commerce plays a major role in customer purchases.
- Efficient delivery and high customer ratings indicate a positive shopping experience.

8. Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory and promotional campaigns for Electronics and Home & Garden products.
2. Continue marketing Sports, Beauty, and Books to maintain high purchase volume.
3. Develop targeted campaigns for female customers and customers aged 25–45 years.
4. Expand marketing efforts in lower-performing cities while strengthening presence in Istanbul, Ankara, and Izmir.
5. Continue improving the mobile shopping experience.
6. Maintain fast delivery and high customer service standards to sustain customer satisfaction.

9. Conclusion

The analysis of 17,049 e-commerce transactions demonstrates strong business performance with high customer satisfaction and healthy sales across multiple product categories. Customer behavior analysis, sales trends, payment preferences, and delivery performance reveal valuable insights that can support strategic business decisions. Implementing the recommended actions can help increase revenue, improve customer retention, and strengthen the company's competitive position in the e-commerce market.
